I am a Systems Engineer at [Northvolt](https://northvolt.com/), where I develop algorithms for battery management systems. 
I completed my M.Sc. in Robotics at [Warsaw University of Technology](https://eng.pw.edu.pl/) in October 2024. My [thesis](https://anurye.github.io/assets/pdf/Mobile_Robot_Navigation_in_Dynamic_Environments.pdf) focused on mobile robot navigation in dynamic environments using deep reinforcement learning, conducted under the supervision of [Prof. Elżbieta Jarzębowska](https://www.meil.pw.edu.pl/daas/DAAS2/People/Elzbieta-Jarzebowska). 
Prior to this, I earned my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from [Addis Ababa Science and Technology University](http://www.aastu.edu.et/) in September 2021.


**Research Interests:**  
I am driven by the challenge of creating robotic agents that go beyond task efficiency to engage naturally and safely with people. Specifically:  
- Developing models to predict human behavior in dynamic, real-world environments with limited training data, to enable robots to respond proactively to subtle behavioral cues.  
- Designing robust evaluation frameworks for safety in HRI, integrating technical reliability with user-centered design principles.  
- Testing and refining these models across diverse robotic platforms and scenarios to ensure adaptability with minimal parameter tuning.
